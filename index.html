About Me

I started my Master programme in Winter 2017 at McGill University under the supervision of Gunter Mussbacher. In late 2017, my program was fast-tracked to PhD, under the supervision of Gunter Musshbacher and Joerg Kienzle. My thesis focuses on the reuse, combination, and maintaining consistencies between modelling languages, including domain-specific languages. Although my background is in Electronic Engineering, I switched to Software Engineering (at McGill) due to my passion for software engineering and programming. Before I joined McGill, I worked at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, as an academic graduate assistant for more than two years. Also, I obtained an MSc in Computer Engineering and a Bachelor of Engineering in Electronic Engineering, both from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, in 2012 and 2017, respectively.
