About Me

Since July 2023, I am working as a postdoctoral fellow at Polytechnique Montreal, where I lead a cloud-based collaborative research between the SWAT Lab at polytechnique Montreal and BusPas Inc., under the supervision of Foutse Khomh. In addition, I am a course instructor at Polytechnique Montreal, where I teach a Software Engineering course (Model-Based SOftware Engineering) for both undergraduate and graduate students. In this course, we collaborate with NASA JPL to build modeling tools, aiming to employ ontological concepts to address system modeling challenges. I hold a PhD in Software Engineering from McGill University.
