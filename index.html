About Me

I am a researcher and educator specializing in Software Engineering, Cloud Computing, and AI-driven Systems. Currently a Postdoctoral Fellow and Instructor at Polytechnique Montreal, where I lead a cloud-based collaborative research project between the SWAT Lab at polytechnique Montreal and BusPas Inc., under the supervision of Prof. Foutse Khomh. I often guide guide students in real-world applications, including a course-project collaboration with NASA JPL to build modeling tools, leveraging ontological concepts to tackle complex system modeling challenges. I earned my Ph.D. in Software Engineering from McGill University, where I advanced methods for model-driven engineering, system integration, and modularity of complex systems. My work bridges research, teaching, and industry collaboration, with a focus on developing scalable, sustainable, maintainable, and intelligent computing solutions while mentoring the next generation of engineers and scientists.
